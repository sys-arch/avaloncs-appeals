Official Appeal Form

Ban Appeal Submission

Complete this form carefully and honestly. Very short, vague, false or low-effort appeals may be denied without further review. Each answer must be detailed enough to help the AvalonCS team understand your case properly.

Before submitting

  • Be respectful and truthful in every answer.
  • Explain what happened clearly and take responsibility where appropriate.
  • Do not use AI-generated, copied or template-style answers.
  • Every field below has a minimum length requirement.

Describe what happened from your point of view. Include the events, your actions, what rule or behaviour may have led to the punishment, and any relevant context.

Minimum 200 characters

Explain why you believe a second chance should be considered. Show reflection, accountability and any change in attitude or understanding since the punishment.

Minimum 250 characters

Give concrete actions, not generic promises. Mention what rules you will follow, what behaviour you will change, and how you will act differently in the future.

Minimum 200 characters

Use this field only for genuinely relevant information: misunderstandings, context that may not be visible in logs, or anything important that helps clarify the case.
