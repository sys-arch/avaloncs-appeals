Official AvalonCS Platform

AvalonCS Appeals

This is the official platform for appeals related to sanctions issued within the AvalonCS Discord community. Here you can request a review of your case through a structured and transparent process.

Login with Discord

You must sign in with your Discord account before submitting or reviewing an appeal.

What is this website?

This website is the official entry point for appeals related to punishments, restrictions or administrative measures issued within AvalonCS' Discord group. It allows users to formally request a review under the rules and internal procedures of the community.

How does it work?

  1. Log in with your Discord account.
  2. Fill in the required information and explain your situation clearly.
  3. Our team reviews the request and issues a response when the evaluation is completed.

What data is collected?

To process your request, we may collect information such as your Discord account identifier, your username, your email address, the text you submit, and technical records needed to protect the platform and verify the legitimacy of the request.

Important information

Submitting an appeal does not guarantee its approval. False, abusive, duplicated or incomplete submissions may be rejected without further action. Please ensure that all information provided is accurate, respectful and relevant to your case.

Before you continue

Please use this platform only for legitimate Discord appeal requests. This system is intended to review sanctions and related administrative actions. General support matters, account changes or unrelated requests may need to be handled through other AvalonCS channels.

Transparency and review

Every appeal is reviewed individually. The information you provide helps the AvalonCS team understand the context of your case and determine whether the original action should be maintained, modified or reversed.
